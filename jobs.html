Oceanographer $132,569 They study the ocean and all the things relating to it, including marine life. There are many different types of oceanographers, such as chemical oceanographers or geological oceanographers. There are many choices, but they all deal with the similar aspect of working with the ocean and it's ecosystem.
  • In the ocean

  • Research Positions
  • Etc
