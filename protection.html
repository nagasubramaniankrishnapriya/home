  • How to protect our sea life and oceans.

Most of our oceans are slowly being polluted due to human activity and such. Most of the animals I have talked about on the page Sea Animals, are endangered, such as the Arctic Seal which is endangered. So how can we make sure these animals and the habitat they live in, are protected?

Protection

There are many ways we can protect our oceans and sea life.

  1. Raise Awareness/Volunteer

    One can volunteer at their local beaches, help cleanup, and also help raise awareness in their community, or at their school. By building communities that will help raise awareness, we can help protect them.

  2. Reduce Waste

    A lot of waste ends up dumped in the oceans. An easy way to avoid this is to reduce your own waste. It starts with you, and through your actions, you can get others to act as well.

  3. Fish responsibly

    Overfishing or over hunting any sort of animal harms their population. Fish only what you need, and continue on. That helps you be sustainable and other people will also have enough fish to fish for themselves, as well as leave time for this species to repopulate.

  4. Support organizations that help this cause

    Organizations like Oceana work to protect the ocean. By supporting these organizations, you can also help create a better environment for the sea life, and a better ocean for all.
